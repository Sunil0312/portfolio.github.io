SYSTEMATIC ANALYSIS OF KIDNEY VOLUME ESTIMATION THROUGH
CYCLIC BAYESIAN NEURAL NETWORK

The study proposes a systematic approach
for estimating kidney volume using a Cyclic
Bayesian Neural Network. This method combines
Bayesian inference and neural networks to
accurately analyze kidney volumes.
